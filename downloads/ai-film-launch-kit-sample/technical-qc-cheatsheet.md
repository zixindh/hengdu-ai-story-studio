# Technical QC Cheat Sheet

Before submission, confirm every value against the current official rules.

- Duration: exact permitted range, including whether credits count.
- Picture: resolution, aspect ratio, frame rate, codec, pixel format, and color space.
- Audio: codec, sample rate, channel layout, peak/loudness limit, and no accidental clipping.
- Captions: language, burned-in versus sidecar, safe area, spelling, and timing.
- Content: no accidental text, logos, watermarks, duplicates, or unlicensed material.
- Delivery: exact filename, file size limit, required platform, link permissions, and expiry.
- Evidence: final SHA-256, prompt/rights ledger, tool/model disclosure, and signed forms.

Test the final link from a private/incognito window. Keep the exact submitted master and its checksum unchanged.
